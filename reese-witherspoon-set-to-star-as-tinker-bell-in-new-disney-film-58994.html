REPORTReese Witherspoon Set to Star as Tinker Bell in New Disney Film

Publish date: 2024-05-23

Bring out the pixie dust!

We are so ready to think happy thoughts and fly after hearing the exciting news that Tinseltown’s sweetheart, Reese Witherspoon, is going to be the next Tinker Bell!

According to People, the blonde beauty is not only going to star in the newly slated live-action film based on the character from Disney’s Peter Pan, she is going to produce it as well —now that’s what we call some serious girl power!
