Donel Mangena performs at Queen's birthday celebrations

Publish date: 2024-07-28

16 year old Donel Mangena put on his biggest performance to date last night, just weeks after finishing as runner up on The Voice UK.

The charismatic performer joined a host of big name acts to perform at The Queen‘s 92nd birthday concert at The Royal Albert Hall, after receiving a letter of invitation from Prince Harry during his time in the competition.

https://www.instagram.com/p/Bh2N2U_jnrd/?utm_source=ig_embed
