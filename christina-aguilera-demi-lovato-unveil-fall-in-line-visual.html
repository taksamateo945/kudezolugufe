Christina Aguilera and Demi Lovato unveil 'Fall In Line' visual

Publish date: 2024-06-22

Christina Aguilera and Demi Lovato have unveiled the music video for their empowering anthem ‘Fall In Line’.

The visual begins with child versions of Christina and Demi innocently playing in a field before they are suddenly kidnapped by a masked, armed duo. As soon as the vocals kick in, we see Christina and Demi being held in a prison and watched by more masked people.

Giving the powerful song even more of a punch, later on in the visual we see the fierce females make their break for freedom. After fighting back against the masked people, Christina and Demi manage to escape, making their way back to the glorious field where the visual began.

Check out the video for ‘Fall In Line’ below.

Following the song’s release on Friday, Christina and Demi delivered the debut TV performance of ‘Fall In Line’ on Sunday during the 2018 Billboard Music Awards. The pair put on a spine-tingling show, taking our breath away with their sensational powerhouse vocals.

‘Fall In Line’ is taken from Christina’s forthcoming album Liberation. The album is due for release on June 15.
