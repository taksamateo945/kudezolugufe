Alicia Silverstone Recreates Iconic 'Clueless' Scene with Son Bear in TikTok Debut - Watch!

Publish date: 2024-04-06

Alicia Silverstone is making her TikTok debut!

On Friday (June 4), the 44-year-old actress shared her first ever TikTok video, in which she recreates one of the most iconic scenes from her 1995 movie Clueless.

PHOTOS: Check out the latest pics of Alicia Silverstone

In the video, Alicia is wearing her character Cher Horowitz’s yellow plaid jacket and is walking down her deck.

At one point, Alicia‘s son Bear, 10, bumps into her. She then pushes him away and yells Cher’s iconic “As if!” line.

Before ending the video, Alicia pulls Bear back into the shot for a big hug.

“Ugh! As if… I wouldn’t join TikTok. ??? #Clueless #AsIf,” Alicia fittingly titled the video.
