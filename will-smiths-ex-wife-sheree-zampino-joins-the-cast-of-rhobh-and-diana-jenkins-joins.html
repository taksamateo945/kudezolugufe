Will Smith's ex-wife Sheree Zampino joins the cast of RHOBH... and Diana Jenkins joins

Publish date: 2024-04-19

Will Smith’s ex-wife Sheree Zampino is joining the cast of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills.

The 54-year-old will be a ‘friend of’ on the show, while another new cast member Diana Jenkins is joining as a ‘full-time’ member, according to E! News.

 Will and Sheree, 54, were married from 1992 until 1995 and share son Trey Smith, 29.

The latest: Will Smith’s ex-wife Sheree Zampino is joining the cast of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills. The 54-year-old will be a ‘friend of’ on the show, while another new cast member Diana Jenkins is joining as a ‘full-time’ member

Sheree and Diana join regular RHOBH cast members Lisa Rinna, Kyle Richards, Erika Jayne, Garcelle Beauvais, Sutton Stracke, Crystal Kung Minkoff, Dorit Kemsley and Kathy Hilton.

Sheree actually appeared in a May 2020 episode as Garcelle’s friend; she also appeared in a 2014 episode as well.

She had a main role in the reality show Hollywood Exes.  

Sheree is an entrepreneur and artist; she has multiple brands under Sheree Elizabeth Inc including a relaunched skincare company called Whoop Ash.
