The Dangers in My Heart Season 2 Episode 3 Release Date: The Dangers in My Heart, originally titled (Boku no Kokoro no Yabai Yatsu) in Japanese, is a manga created by Norio Sakurai. This series, which began in March 2018 in Weekly Shōnen Champion and later moved to Champion Cross and Manga Cross, has been compiled into nine tankōbon volumes. Additionally, an anime adaptation by Shin-Ei Animation was broadcast from April to June 2023, accompanied by a short spin-off original net animation (ONA) released on December 10, 2023. The second season of the anime premiered in January 2024.

As of now, fans are eagerly anticipating The Dangers in My Heart Season 2 Episode 3. They are curious about its release date, the upcoming storyline, and other details. In this article, we aim to provide all the available information about The Dangers in My Heart Season 2 Episode 3. So, if you’re interested in learning more about the next episode, continue reading the entire article.

The third episode of Season 2 of The Dangers in My Heart will be out for fans around the world on Saturday, January 20, 2024. For viewers in Japan, it will be accessible on Sunday, January 21, 2024, starting at 1:30 am JST.

Who Is in The Voice Cast of The Dangers in My Heart Season 2?

The second season will see the return of many familiar faces from Season 1. Hiroaki Akagi, the director known for Teasing Master Takagi-san, will be back, along with Jukki Hanada as the scriptwriter, Masato Katsumata as the character designer, and Kensuke Ushio from Chainsaw Man as the music composer. The production will once again be handled by Shin-Ei Animation.

Here’s a look at the voice cast for Season 2:

  • Kyotaro Ichikawa: Shun Horie
  • Anna Yamada: Hina Youmiya
  • Chihiro Kobayashi: Ayaka Asai
  • Moeko Sekine: Megumi Han
  • Serina Yoshida: Atsumi Tanezaki
  • Kanna Ando: Yuka Iguchi
  • Yurine Hanzawa: Reina Ueda

In addition to these familiar voices, the opening theme song for the new season will be “Bokura wa Sore wo Ai to Yonda” performed by Atarayo. The closing theme, titled “Koi Shiteru Jibun Sura Aiserunda,” will be sung by Kohana Lam, who also performed the closing theme for Season 1.

What Can We Expect from The Dangers in My Heart Season 2 Episode 3?

In Episode 2, Yamada’s character was thoroughly explored, revealing her past struggles and the way her family treated her during those difficult times. This provided viewers with a deeper understanding of her, making it easier to empathize with her. Contrary to her usual cheerful demeanor, Yamada showed a more vulnerable side when she was with the right person, allowing the audience to see a different facet of her personality.

Notably, Ichikawa took the initiative this time, providing comfort and support to Yamada. Towards the end of the episode, Yamada expressed her excitement about Ichikawa’s personal growth. She even playfully whispered to him, asking him to call her name frequently. This showcased a strengthening bond between them, suggesting a positive trajectory for their relationship. As the episode concluded on a positive note, the next installment will likely continue to unfold as a heartwarming and joyful story for these two characters.
