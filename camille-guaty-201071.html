Actress Camille Guaty Engaged! | Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-04-13

So who else got engaged over the holidays? Actress Camille Guaty — who's starred on TV's Las Vegas, Prison Break and Cupid, the Jen Garner flick Ghosts of Girlfriends Past and more — confirms her engagement exclusively to UsMagazine.com!

British songwriter Sy Rhys Kaye proposed to Guaty, 31, over the holidays in Los Angeles, a source tells Us. "She met Sy a few months ago," the pal notes, "and it was love at first sight. She went to visit him in London, and he proposed when he came back to visit L.A.."
