His Journey To NFL

Jalen attended Waxahachie High School and played both football and track & field there. He enrolled at Texas Christian University in Fort Worth, Texas, to play college football. He played for TCU for three seasons from 2017 to 2019.

The Philadelphia Eagles selected him in the first round with the 21st overall pick in the 2020 NFL Draft. On 20 July 2020, he signed a 4-year contract with the team, worth $13.3 million.
